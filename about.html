Who We Are

Electrical Excellence Since 2004

Stackly Electrical Services was founded in 2004 with a clear mission: to deliver world-class electrical engineering solutions that power the future. Over the past two decades, we've grown from a small local team into a nationally recognized electrical services provider.

We specialize in industrial, commercial, and residential electrical systems — from complex factory automation and control panel design to smart home wiring and emergency electrical repairs.

Our team of 50+ certified engineers and technicians is committed to safety, precision, and delivering projects on time, every time. We hold certifications from NECA, IBEW, and OSHA, ensuring every installation meets the highest safety standards.
